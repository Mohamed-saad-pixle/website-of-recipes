One-Pot Spaghetti (Lunch/Dinner)

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, oregano to taste
  • Optional: grated cheese, fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add the spaghetti, water, tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and oregano.
  3. Stir, bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer. Cover and cook 10–12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. When the spaghetti is soft and sauce is thickened, remove from heat.
  5. Top with cheese or basil if you like!
