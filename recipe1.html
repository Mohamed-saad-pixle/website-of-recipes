. Scrambled Eggs with Veggies (Breakfast)

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl, add milk (optional), salt, pepper, and beat well.
  2. Heat the butter/oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add onion, tomato, and bell pepper. Sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the eggs and stir gently until just set.
  5. Serve warm with bread or toast.
